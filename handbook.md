## The main entry point to the Pyragogy Handbook — AI Edition

This page is the central entry point to the **Pyragogy Handbook — AI Edition**.

It provides the structural map of the handbook as it currently stands: not as a finished book, but as a stabilized editorial framework for an ongoing process of human–AI co-learning.

The purpose of this page is simple: to help readers understand where they are, how the handbook is organized, and where each layer of work belongs.

## What this page does

This page does not contain the full theory of Pyragogy.

It functions as a structural index: a readable map of the handbook’s main sections, their role, and their relationship to one another.

It is a page of orientation, not completion.

## Editorial role

The handbook is built as a frontstage.

This means that what appears here has already passed through a process of selection, shaping, and stabilization. Drafts, exploratory notes, raw co-editing, and unresolved debate belong to the backstage and are intentionally kept separate.

The structure presented here is therefore editorial rather than merely technical.

It is designed to support clarity, navigation, and future growth.

## Structural principle

The handbook is not organized as a flat collection of unrelated pages.

It is organized as a layered work.

Each part serves a different function:

- some pages explain
- some pages define
- some pages operationalize
- some pages document experiments
- some pages preserve uncertainty

This layered structure is essential. Without it, a living handbook collapses into accumulation.

## Current stage

The handbook is still in its early formation stage.

At this stage, the main priority is not expansion but structural integrity.

The goal is to establish a clear editorial skeleton that can later support deeper writing, collaborative revision, and human–AI development without losing coherence.

## Orientation statement

Readers entering the handbook should understand one thing clearly:

This is not a finished doctrine.  
It is a structured exploration.

Its stability is provisional, editorial, and intentional.

Its deeper movement remains alive.
