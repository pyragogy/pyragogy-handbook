## Why an AI Edition exists

Peer learning changes when AI enters the process not only as an instrument, but as a cognitive participant.
This edition exists to document that shift with conceptual precision and practical honesty.

## What makes this handbook different

This is not a static book and not an unfiltered stream of notes.
It is a living handbook: dynamic in method, stable in publication.

## What is visible here

Public pages in this handbook are stabilized versions.
They are the result of drafting, revision, disagreement, and editorial choice.

## What is not visible here

Raw drafts, collaborative pads, unresolved fragments, and unreviewed AI output do not belong to the public frontstage.
They belong to the backstage and the canonical working archive.

## Editorial promise

A page appears here only when it becomes readable, defensible, and useful as part of a larger whole.
