# What is Pyragogy

> **Type:** Concept Note  
> **Status:** Stable  
> **Version:** v0.1  
> **Last synthesized:** 2026-04-01  
> **Reviewed by:** Human  
> **Open tensions:** 0

## Definition

Pyragogy is an exploration of human–AI co-learning in which AI is approached not merely as an instrument, but as a cognitive participant.
It is not a closed doctrine, but an evolving framework.

## Why it matters

The term names a shift in educational and epistemic practice.
It makes visible forms of interaction that are poorly described by the language of automation, assistance, or simple tool use.

## What it is not

Pyragogy is not the glorification of AI.
It is not frictionless productivity.
It is not the replacement of human judgment.

## Current direction

The framework investigates rhythm, divergence, synchronization, cognitive friction, editorial stabilization, and the design of structures where humans and AI can learn together without collapsing difference.
