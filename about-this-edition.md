# About this Edition

> **Abstract**
> This is the AI ​​edition of the Pyragogy Handbook: a living handbook produced through human-AI collaboration. It documents the transition from traditional peer learning to human-AI peer production, making explicit Pyragogy patterns, methods, cases, and technologies.

## Context
Peer learning changes radically when AI is no longer just a tool, but becomes a full-fledged cognitive participant. Learning groups must manage momentum, shared memory, mixed feedback, and real-time co-writing. This edition exists to document that shift with conceptual precision and practical honesty, without obscuring unresolved tensions.

## What makes this handbook different
- It is not a static book.
- It is not a raw flow of notes.
- It is a living handbook: dynamic in its method, stable in its public publication.

## What is visible here (frontstage)
Public pages are stabilized versions: the result of drafting, review, disagreement, and editorial choice.

They represent the point at which content becomes readable, defensible, and useful within the entire system.

## What's NOT visible here (backstage)
- Rough drafts
- Collaborative pads
- Unresolved fragments
- Unreviewed AI outputs

These remain in the canonical working archive and in co-writing tools (n8n, pads, AI agents).

## How to use this handbook (Instructions/Walkthrough)
1. Start from the **Home** to orient yourself in the 4 pillars.
2. Use the **Glossary** to clarify basic terms.
3. Navigate the **Patterns** and **Methods** (Part III) to apply immediate solutions.
4. Read the **Case Studies** (Part IV) to see Pyragogy in action.
5. Explore the **Technologies** (Part V) to build your co-learning environment.

## Editorial Promise
A page appears here only when it is **readable, defensible, and useful** as part of a larger whole.

The process is transparent: each page includes its version, summary date, and revision status.
